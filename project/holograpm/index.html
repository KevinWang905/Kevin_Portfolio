In Line Holography

Mar 30, 2022

A hologram is a method of optical imagery, where, as opposed to a classic photograph which captures the intensities of an object, a hologram captures the intensities and phase of an object. That is to say, a hologram captures information regarding the entire object, and can thus reconstruct a true 3D representation of that object.

The goal of this project is to construct an in line holographic imaging setup and capture amplitude and phase information for an object. After this capture is complete, the hologram will be digitally reconstructed, and displayed on a monitor to illustrate multiple focal lengths with a single image capture.
